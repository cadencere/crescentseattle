About

A small building in Columbia City.

Crescent is a 37-unit, three-story building on 42nd Ave S. Studios, one-bedrooms, and two-bedrooms. The whole building was renovated in 2026 with new kitchens, baths, and finishes.

Columbia City station is four blocks away. Downtown is about 15 minutes by Link. The Columbia City business district, with the bakery, theater, farmers market, and most of the restaurants, is a five-minute walk.
